Happiness is that feeling that comes over you when you know life's good and you can't help but smile!

In the year 2015, the happiest country in the world was Switzerland while the saddest country was Togo. 19 out of 25 people were happy in Switzerland but a mere 7 out of 25 people were happy in Togo.
