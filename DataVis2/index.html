Quality of Life...

The visualisation on the right displays the life expectancies of residents throughout the world. Happiness often leads to humans living a better quality of life; hence having better health.

This can be suggested as seen on the chart on the right. People residing in countries that have a higher happiness score tend to have life expectancies that are longer than counties ranked below them.

Furthermore...

Countries with a higher happiness score tend to have more family. This can be seen on the chart on the left as countries with a lower happiness score tend to have a lesser number of family members.
